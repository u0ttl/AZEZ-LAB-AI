IMPORTANT BOUNDARY
V12 performs real OCR on image reports and text extraction on text-bearing PDFs.
Scanned multi-page PDFs are detected but are NOT falsely claimed as fully OCR processed.
A PDF page-rendering worker is still required for robust scanned-PDF OCR and page-specific bounding boxes.

OCR output is not automatically a confirmed measured fact. Low-confidence/incomplete fields are routed to verification.
